Health Benefits

Rich in Monounsaturated Fatty Acids

  • Mustard oil is high in monounsaturated fatty acids, which are considered heart-healthy and may help lower bad cholesterol levels.

Contains Omega-3 Fatty Acids

  • It contains alpha-linolenic acid (ALA), a type of omega-3 fatty acid that is beneficial for heart health and may reduce inflammation.

Antibacterial Properties

  • Mustard oil has natural antibacterial and antifungal properties, which may help fight infections when applied topically.

Anti-inflammatory

  • Massaging mustard oil onto sore muscles or joints may help reduce inflammation and provide relief from pain.
